How to Play Werewolf

Game Overview:

  • Total Players: 60 (divided into 4 groups of 15)
  • Goal: Villagers eliminate werewolves, Werewolves eliminate villagers

Roles:

  • Villagers: Identify and vote out werewolves during the day
  • Werewolves: Kill villagers at night, vote during the day
  • Seer: Can see a player's role each night
  • Doctor: Can protect a player each night

Game Phases:

  • Night: Werewolves choose a victim
  • Day: All players vote to eliminate someone
  • End: Game ends when one side wins
